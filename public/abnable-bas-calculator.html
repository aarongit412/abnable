How lenders assess self-employed income differently
🏦
Major banks (net profit): Use your tax return net profit after all expenses. High deductions = lower assessed income = smaller loan.
⚡
Specialist lenders (gross revenue): Use your total BAS turnover before expenses. More accurately reflects what you actually earn.
✚
Addbacks (Pepper Money): Add depreciation, interest, and one-off costs back to your net profit — increases assessable income significantly.
